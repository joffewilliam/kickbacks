# Kickbacks Product Notes

## Register

product

## Users

Kickbacks is for developers who are willing to run AI CLI sessions inside a standalone terminal app in exchange for a share of sponsor revenue. They care about keeping their terminal work private, using normal shells and CLIs, and understanding exactly when earning mode is active.

## Product Purpose

Kickbacks is a desktop terminal for ad-supported AI developer workflows. The app owns the terminal UI, login state, sponsor placements, and payout settings. It does not upload terminal output, prompts, commands, project paths, or files.

## Brand Personality

Plainspoken, private, payment-aware, and developer-native. The app should feel like a focused terminal tool with payout controls, not a social feed, crypto dashboard, or intrusive ad product.

## Product Principles

- Blank terminal first, smart earning controls second.
- Provider eligibility is explicit and user-controlled in v1.
- Ad events contain only sanitized placement metadata.
- Keep the permanent terminal transcript visually clean.
- Make payout and privacy state easy to inspect without leaving the workflow.
